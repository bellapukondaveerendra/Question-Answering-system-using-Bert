Signs and symptoms of breast cancer may include :
Breast and nipple changes can be a sign of breast cancer. Make an appointment with your doctor if you notice anything unusual.
The symptoms of breast cancer are A breast lump or thickening that feels different from the surrounding tissue, Change in the size, shape or appearance of a breast, Changes to the skin over the breast, such as dimpling,A newly inverted nipple, Peeling, scaling, crusting or flaking of the pigmented area of skin surrounding the nipple (areola) or breast skin and Redness or pitting of the skin over your breast like the skin of an orange.
